# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We want Pixel Office to be a welcoming, harassment-free space for everyone, regardless
of experience level, background, or identity.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful and constructive. Assume good faith.
- Welcome newcomers and questions; critique ideas, not people.
- Accept feedback gracefully and give it kindly.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks.
- Trolling, insults, or sustained disruption.
- Publishing others' private information without consent.

## Scope

This applies in all project spaces — issues, pull requests, discussions — and when
representing the project publicly.

## Enforcement

Report unacceptable behavior to the maintainer via a private channel (see
[`SECURITY.md`](SECURITY.md) for how to reach out privately). Reports will be reviewed
in good faith; maintainers may edit, remove, or reject contributions and comments that
violate this code, and may block repeat or serious offenders.

This is adapted, in spirit, from the [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org).
